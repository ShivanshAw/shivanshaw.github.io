Unsent

A whimsical 3D platformer where you play as Valentine, a heart-headed postman delivering letters that were never sent.

Project Type: University project → Studio project
Year: 2024–2025
Role: UI, Enemy AI, Dialogue & Interaction Systems
Tech: Unreal Engine, Blueprints, C++, Behavior Trees

Overview

Unsent is a whimsical 3D platformer inspired by Psychonauts 2 and Mario, set in a playful world filled with characters, stories, and forgotten emotions.

You play as Valentine, a heart-headed postman working in the Unsentiment Department. Your job is to deliver letters that were never delivered, uncovering personal stories as you explore this expressive, story-driven world.

Players meet strange and memorable characters, collect stamps, and fill out a personal journal while navigating platforming challenges and narrative interactions.

Technical Contributions

Designed and implemented enemy behaviors using Unreal Engine Behavior Trees, supporting modular state transitions, patrol logic, and reactive combat behavior.
Built the dialogue and interaction framework from scratch, enabling branching conversations, character responses, and narrative-driven interactions throughout the world.
Developed UI systems for the journal, stamp collection, and player feedback, ensuring clarity and cohesion with the game’s whimsical tone.
